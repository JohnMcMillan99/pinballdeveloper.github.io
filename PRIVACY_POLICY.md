# Privacy Policy for PinLevel

**Effective Date:** March 17, 2026

## Overview

PinLevel is a pinball machine leveling tool that uses your device's accelerometer sensor to measure pitch and roll angles. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

## Data Collection

PinLevel does **not** collect, transmit, store on remote servers, or share any personal data. Specifically:

- **No personal information** is collected (no name, email, location, or device identifiers).
- **No analytics or tracking** services are used.
- **No network requests** are made by the app. PinLevel works entirely offline.
- **No advertising** SDKs or third-party services are included.

## Data Stored on Your Device

PinLevel stores the following data **locally on your device only**, using Android DataStore preferences:

- **Calibration offsets** (pitch and roll sensor corrections)
- **Machine profiles** (pinball machine names and target leveling angles)
- **App preferences** (voice guide settings, glass offset settings)

This data never leaves your device. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is deleted.

## Sensor Access

PinLevel accesses your device's **accelerometer sensor** solely to calculate tilt angles for leveling purposes. Sensor readings are processed in real time and are not recorded or stored.

## Children's Privacy

PinLevel does not knowingly collect any data from children or any other users. The app collects no data at all.
